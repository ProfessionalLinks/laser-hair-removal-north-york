What is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal is a non-invasive procedure that uses concentrated light to penetrate the hair follicle. The pigment in the follicle absorbs the light, destroying the hair from its root. This process significantly reduces hair growth, making it a preferred choice for long-term hair removal. Compared to traditional methods such as shaving, waxing, or tweezing, laser hair removal provides a more permanent solution.

What to Expect During Treatment

The laser hair removal process typically involves several sessions to achieve optimal results. Each session lasts from a few minutes to an hour, depending on the size of the treatment area. Patients may feel a slight discomfort, similar to a snapping rubber band, but it’s generally well-tolerated.

After treatment, it’s important to avoid sun exposure and use sunscreen, as the skin may be more sensitive. It's also crucial to follow the aftercare instructions provided by the clinic to avoid complications such as irritation or pigmentation changes.

Laser Hair Removal Costs in North York

The cost of laser hair removal in North York can vary depending on the treatment area, the number of sessions required, and the clinic chosen. On average, smaller areas like the upper lip or underarms cost less, while larger areas like the legs or back may be more expensive. Many clinics offer packages or discounts for multiple sessions.
